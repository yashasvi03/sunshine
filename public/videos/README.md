# Birthday Video Message

Place your birthday video message here.

## File Name

`birthday-message.mp4`

## Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps
- **Length**: 1-3 minutes (2 minutes ideal)
- **File Size**: <50MB (use compression if needed)
- **Audio**: Clear, good quality
- **Orientation**: Landscape

## Recording Tips

1. **Lighting**: Use natural light or a well-lit room
2. **Background**: Clean, not distracting
3. **Camera**: Phone camera in landscape mode is perfect
4. **Stability**: Use a tripod or prop your phone steady
5. **Look at lens**: Look directly at the camera lens, not the screen
6. **Audio**: Record in a quiet environment
7. **Distance**: About 3-4 feet from camera

## Content Suggestions

- Start: "Happy 26th Birthday, [Her Name]"
- Express what this birthday means to you
- Share favorite moments from the past year
- What you love about her at 26
- Your hopes/dreams for her year ahead
- Maybe include funny moments or inside jokes
- End: Lead into the gift reveal

## Video Compression Tools

If your video is too large (>50MB):
- [HandBrake](https://handbrake.fr/) - Free, powerful compression
- [CloudConvert](https://cloudconvert.com/) - Online converter
- iMovie / Windows Video Editor - Built-in compression

## Optional: Video Thumbnail

Create a thumbnail image named `thumbnail.jpg` (1920x1080) to show before the video plays.

---

**Important**: Practice your message first! You want to speak from the heart but have a general idea of what you'll say.
